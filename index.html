Services

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is based on a single premise: we experience symptoms because energy is not moving freely through our body. Translated to modern western terms, acupuncture treatment causes your body to release hormones that regulate its various systems and promote healing.

Chiropractic

Doctors of Chiropractic focus on the nerve system,(the brain and spinal cord) which manages the body's vast chemical interactions to ensure proper function. The brain sends messages through the spinal cord across a huge network of spinal nerves to deliver information to every cell, organ and system of the body.

Massage Therapy

Our integrated massage is a fusion of eastern and western modalities such as Swedish, Deep Tissue, Sports, Orthopedic, Shiatsu, Reflexology, and Cranio Sacral Therapies.

DOMINIC QUIN-HARKIN, CMT Certified and licensed in massage since 2008, Dominic is a San Rafael native and a well-balanced healer providing massage, energy balancing and life coaching in the SF Bay Area. He combines physical and emotional, western and eastern, medical and spiritual. Before massage, his background includes being a 4-year college athlete (UC San Diego Men’s Water Polo), NYC-Trained in Dance (jazz, tap, ballet), and 8+ years of coaching (competitive swimming, water polo) from young ages to high school and college level. He has been practicing bio-energy balancing for 16 years, which focuses on psycho-somatic trauma release, clearing blocks and balancing chi. Dominic has been a lead therapist and trainer at Elements Massage in Greenbrae and has worked in spas in both Park City and Deer Valley, UT, as well as in celebrity gifting suites for the Sundance Film Festival and the Academy Awards. His customized massages operate under the motto “muscles should be coerced and not bullied.” His deep tissue is patient without being forceful and painful. Sessions include helping clients gain knowledge of stretch, warm-up, and workout routines. Being a “Gentle Giant”, Dominic caters to all types of clients ranging from Sports Injuries and Rehab to the stress-relief rejuvenation and pampering. Regardless of the modality used, the end result is feeling balanced and lighter, not the soreness commonly associated with deep massage. His featured modalities include Swedish, Deep Tissue, Sports Therapy, Myofascial Release, Craniosacral, Prenatal, Reflexology, Lymphatic, and Reiki and Chakra Balancing.
